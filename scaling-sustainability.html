Scaling Sustainability

Measuring product carbon footprint across the supply chain.

I was part of the core team that saw the product go from a PoC to
a fully commercialised project. From sketches, to collaboration, workshops
and visual design. This project is so close to my heart!

Problem statement

Building a software that would help manufacturing companies calculate their Product's carbon footprint along the supply chain, and ensuring that the shared data was not only transparent, trusted, but also remained confidential.
