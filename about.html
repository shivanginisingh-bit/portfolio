about me

Hi! I'm based out of Bangalore.

I've been designing experiences for over 12 years across enterprise and consumer products. My focus is turning complexity into clarity—building AI-assisted workflows, scalable design systems, and journeys that feel effortless for people and effective for business.

I believe experiences can be delightful while being functional. I enjoy the full stack of design, but my strengths are empathy, collaboration, and divergent thinking. When I'm not at my desk, you'll find me reading, illustrating, doing yoga, aqua-scaping, or learning to shoot videos—anything that keeps me curious and connected to the world around me.
